D2C acquisition system

Problem: Campaigns were generating traffic, but scaling was limited by unstable CPA and weak creative learning.

Action: Rebuilt the account around funnel stages, creative angles, audience testing and weekly performance decisions.

Result: Stronger acquisition visibility with ROAS, CPA, CTR and revenue as the primary KPIs.

Key learning: Meta Ads scale best when creative testing, landing-page clarity and offer economics are reviewed together.

GOOGLE ADS

High-intent demand capture

Problem: Paid search spend was leaking through low-intent queries and unclear conversion tracking.

Action: Cleaned keyword intent, improved ad-to-page relevance, tightened tracking and prioritized commercial search terms.

Result: Better visibility into conversions, cost per lead, CPC and conversion rate.

Key learning: Google Ads efficiency improves when the account is managed around intent quality, not just traffic volume.

Lifecycle and workflow efficiency
